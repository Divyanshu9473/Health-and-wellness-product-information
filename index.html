About Forever Living Products

Forever Living Products (FLP) is a global leader in natural wellness and personal care, founded in 1978 in the USA. With over 45 years of experience, FLP is present in more than 160 countries, providing 100% Aloe Vera-based health, wellness, and skincare products.

Joining FLP gives you a chance to build your own business with minimal investment. You get access to high-quality, certified products, training, and mentorship from experienced leaders.

Your mentor: Divyanshu Kushwaha – guiding new members to start their FLP journey successfully.
